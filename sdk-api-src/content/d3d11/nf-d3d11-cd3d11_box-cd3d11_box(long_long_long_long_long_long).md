# CD3D11_BOX::CD3D11_BOX(LONG,LONG,LONG,LONG,LONG,LONG)


## -description

Instantiates a new instance of a <a href="/previous-versions/windows/desktop/legacy/jj151614(v=vs.85)">CD3D11_BOX</a> structure that is initialized with the dimensions of a box.

## -parameters

### -param Left

Type: <b>LONG</b>

The x-coordinate of the left hand side  of the box.

### -param Top

Type: <b>LONG</b>

The y-coordinate of the top of the box.

### -param Front

Type: <b>LONG</b>

The z-coordinate of the front of the box.

### -param Right

Type: <b>LONG</b>

The x-coordinate of the right hand side of the box.

### -param Bottom

Type: <b>LONG</b>

The y-coordinate of the bottom of the box.

### -param Back

Type: <b>LONG</b>

The y-coordinate of the back of the box.

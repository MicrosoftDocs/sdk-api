# D3DX11_SCAN_OPCODE enumeration


## -description


Scan opcodes.


## -enum-fields




### -field D3DX11_SCAN_OPCODE_ADD

Add values.


### -field D3DX11_SCAN_OPCODE_MIN

Take the minimum value.


### -field D3DX11_SCAN_OPCODE_MAX

Take the maximum value.


### -field D3DX11_SCAN_OPCODE_MUL

Multiply the values.


### -field D3DX11_SCAN_OPCODE_AND

Perform a logical AND on the values.


### -field D3DX11_SCAN_OPCODE_OR

Perform a logical OR on the values.


### -field D3DX11_SCAN_OPCODE_XOR

Perform a logical XOR on the values.

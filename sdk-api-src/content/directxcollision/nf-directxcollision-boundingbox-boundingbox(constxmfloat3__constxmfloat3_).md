# BoundingBox::BoundingBox(const XMFLOAT3 &,const XMFLOAT3 &)


## -description


Creates an instance of the <code>BoundingBox</code> class.


## -parameters




### -param center [in, ref]

The coordinate of the center of the box.


### -param extents [in, ref]

The extents of the box.


## -remarks
